NEPTUNE

Neptune is the farthest planet from the Sun and is known for its deep blue color, supersonic winds, and storm systems like the Great Dark Spot.

Neptune has a small rocky core surrounded by water, methane, and ammonia ices, with a thick outer atmosphere.

As a gas giant, Neptune has no solid surface. Its atmosphere is dynamic with powerful storms and high-speed winds.
