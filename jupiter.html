JUPITER

Jupiter is the largest planet in our solar system. It's a gas giant with a powerful magnetic field and dozens of moons.

Jupiter has a small rocky core surrounded by a deep layer of metallic hydrogen and an outer layer of molecular hydrogen.

Jupiter has no solid surface. Its outer layer is composed of thick clouds with bands and the Great Red Spot storm.
