MARS

Mars is the fourth planet from the Sun and is known as the Red Planet. It has the tallest volcano and deepest canyon in the solar system.

Mars has a core made of iron, nickel, and sulfur, surrounded by a silicate mantle and crust.

The surface has rocky plains, enormous volcanoes like Olympus Mons, and the vast canyon Valles Marineris.
