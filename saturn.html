SATURN

Saturn is the second-largest planet and is well known for its spectacular ring system, made up of ice and rock.

Saturn has a core of iron and nickel surrounded by metallic hydrogen and a thick outer layer of gas.

Like Jupiter, Saturn has no solid surface. Its visible bands and storms form in the outer gaseous layers.
