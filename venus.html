VENUS

Venus is the second planet from the Sun and is similar in structure and size to Earth, but with a thick, toxic atmosphere that traps heat. It is the hottest planet in the solar system.

Venus has a central iron core and a rocky mantle, similar to the composition of Earth. However, its lack of plate tectonics makes its internal heat escape differently.

The surface of Venus is covered with volcanic plains and has large highland regions. It features more volcanoes than any other planet.
