EARTH

Third planet from the Sun and the only known planet to harbor life. About 29.2% of Earth’s surface is land with remaining 70.8% covered with water. Earth’s distance from the Sun, physical properties and geological history have allowed life to evolve and thrive.

Earth has a layered internal structure: a solid inner core, a liquid outer core, a viscous mantle, and a solid crust. The movement of the liquid outer core generates Earth’s magnetic field.

Earth's surface features mountains, valleys, plateaus, and ocean basins. Plate tectonics continuously reshape the surface, causing earthquakes and volcanic activity.
